About Me

Who I Am

I'm Amenyo, a passionate software developer based in Accra, Ghana, with a mission to bridge the digital divide across Africa through innovative technology solutions. With expertise in mobile app development, web development, and AI integration, I help businesses transform their operations and reach new heights.

My journey in software development began with a fascination for how technology can solve real-world problems. Over the years, I've specialized in creating mobile applications using Flutter and React Native, building responsive websites with modern frameworks, and implementing AI solutions that drive business intelligence.

What sets me apart is my deep understanding of the Ghanaian and African market, combined with international development standards. I don't just write code – I create digital experiences that resonate with local users while meeting global quality benchmarks.
